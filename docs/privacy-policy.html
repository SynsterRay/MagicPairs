Privacy Policy — Magic Pairs

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Overview

Magic Pairs is a memory card game developed by Mateusz Bajak. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Magic Pairs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No location data is collected
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No internet connection is required to play

Local Storage

The app stores game data (leaderboard scores and player names) locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Magic Pairs is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. The app is fully offline and contains no user accounts, social features, or external links.

Third-Party Services

Magic Pairs does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
